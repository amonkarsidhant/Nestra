# Nestra Product Brief

## Problem
Smart-home capability has outpaced smart-home usability.

Users today face integration sprawl, fragile automation logic, and inconsistent interfaces. Existing systems are powerful but often engineer-first, not household-first.

## Product
Nestra is an AI-assisted home operations layer that abstracts control complexity while enforcing policy and auditability.

Nestra does not replace device ecosystems. It orchestrates them through one trusted interaction model.

## Who It Serves
- **Households:** simple, safe, explainable daily control
- **Installers:** repeatable setup and maintainable operations
- **Partners/brands:** white-label-ready operating surface

## Core Promise
"Tell your home what outcome you want. Nestra executes safely, explains what happened, and keeps an auditable trail."

## Product Differentiator
Compared to DIY-first platforms, Nestra emphasizes:
1. policy-enforced execution,
2. auditable outcomes,
3. multi-home operational model,
4. premium conversational UX.

## Non-goals (for MVP)
- Building a full replacement for Home Assistant device integration breadth
- Building an advanced visual automation editor
- Supporting every smart-home protocol directly in v1

## Success Criteria (MVP)
- Users can complete core household actions with less friction than existing dashboard-first flows
- Critical actions show visible guardrail checks and explicit outcomes
- Every action is traceable in audit feed
- Users report clear trust in behavior and failure handling
